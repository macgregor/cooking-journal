# Greek Dressing

## Ingredients
* 1 c extra virgin olive oil
* 1/2 c red wine vinegar
* 4 cloves garlic (minced or pressed)
* 1 tbsp dried oregano
* 2 tsp Dijon mustard
* 1 1/2 tsp salt
* 1 tsp black pepper
* 1 tsp agave nectar or sugar


## Instructions
1. Whisk together all of the dressing ingredients until emulsified.
